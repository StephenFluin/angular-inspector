Privacy policy

1. INTRODUCTION

1.1. NOTICE CONCERNING CHILDREN. PLEASE NOTE: We do not direct any of our content specifically at children under 13 years of age. We understand and are committed to respecting the sensitive nature of children’s privacy online. If we learn or have reason to suspect that a Site user is under age 13, we will promptly delete any personal information in that user’s account.

2. INFORMATION COLLECTION PRACTICES

2.1. INFORMATION COLLECTED & USED (a) OPT-IN. Users can opt-in to the collection information regarding Angular domains you discover via use of the Angular Inspector extension, this information is not personally identifiable. The extension can be opt-ed out of data collection or uninstalled at any time.

3. USE AND SHARING OF INFORMATION

3.1. WHAT IS DONE WITH COLLECTED INFORMATION?

(a) If the user opts-in to sharing of domain details, this information will be used to identify Angular applications on the internet.

(b) DATA RETENTION. We will retain opt-in non-personally identifiable domain information indefinitely

5. POLICY UPDATES

5.1. UPDATES AND CHANGES. We may update this privacy statement to reflect changes to our information practices. If we make any material changes we will notify you by email (sent to the e-mail address specified in your account) or by means of a notice on this Site prior to the change becoming effective. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.
